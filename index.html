Tectonic style

During the Jurassic period, Pangaea began to break up. There was a lot of plate movement, and there was a lot of volcanic activity, and a lot of mountain building events. Island arcs were formed near subduction zones, and some attached to continents

Composition of the atmosphere

There was significantly more carbon dioxide during this time, 2-5 times higher than the present. However, the oxygen levels are believed to have been similar as to what they are today.

Temperature

The temperature during the Jurassic period was hotter than it is today, estimating 9-18 degrees farenheit. It was most likely hot and dry, with lots of areas shpowing tropical conditions.

Composition of the Biosphere

The jurassic is around the time when flowering plants first appeared, which are angiosperms. Obviously there was a lot of dinosaurs. There were huge sauropods and theropods. In the ocean, there were lots of corals, brachiopods, bivalves, and other invertebrates.
